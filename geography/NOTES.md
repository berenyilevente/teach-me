# Working Notes

## User preferences
- Practical over academic — always tie facts to news/conversation, never to theory.
- Region-by-region progression after the global skeleton.
- Likes short lessons. Keep within working memory: one tangible win each.

## Teaching approach for this topic
- Lesson = knowledge (in the HTML) + delegate the repetitive map-clicking drill to Seterra.
- Use retrieval practice in every lesson (recall, not recognition where possible).
- Space and interleave older regions into later quizzes so they don't fade.
- Geography facts are factual — cross-check capitals/flags against the CIA World Factbook,
  don't trust memory alone for anything non-obvious.

## Roadmap (tentative, region by region)
1. The World Skeleton — 7 continents + 5 oceans  ← lesson 0001 (DONE)
2. Europe — countries & placement
3. Europe — capitals
4. (then) Middle East (news-heavy), East Asia, Americas, Africa, etc.
- Flags woven in region by region, not as a separate marathon.
