# Practical World Geography — Resources

## Knowledge

- [CIA World Factbook](https://www.cia.gov/the-world-factbook/)
  Authoritative, free, public-domain country profiles for 260+ entities: location,
  neighbours, capital, population, plus the flag and its symbolism. Use for: ground-truth
  facts about any country, flag images, and regional maps. This is our source of truth.
- [CIA World Factbook — Flags of the World](https://www.cia.gov/the-world-factbook/references/flags-of-the-world/)
  Every national flag in one place, with the story behind each design. Use for: flag lessons.
- [BBC Country Profiles](https://www.bbc.com/news/world)
  Plain-language overviews tying each country to its current affairs. Use for: the
  news-and-context layer — why a country is in the headlines.

## Skills / Practice

- [Seterra (on GeoGuessr)](https://www.geoguessr.com/vgp/3007)
  The best free interactive map-quiz tool since 1997. 400+ quizzes: continents, countries,
  capitals, flags, oceans — click the map, get instant feedback. Use for: the retrieval
  drills after every lesson. This is where the knowledge becomes durable.
  - Continents & Oceans: https://www.geoguessr.com/quiz/seterra (search "continents")
  - Europe: Countries — https://www.geoguessr.com/vgp/3007
  - Europe: Capitals — https://www.geoguessr.com/vgp/3225

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [r/geography](https://www.reddit.com/r/geography/)
  Large, active community for questions, maps, and "where is this / why is this here" discussion.
  Use for: curiosity-driven questions and testing your mental map against others.
