# Starting point: ~750 blitz / ~950 rapid, blunders & focus are the bottleneck

Established 2026-06-15 from the learner's own description. Levente plays online chess at
roughly 750 blitz and 950 rapid. Self-reported core weakness: **frequently hangs pieces,
misses obvious captures, and loses focus / plays on autopilot during games.**

Why it matters for future sessions: at this level, blunder-rate dominates results far more
than opening knowledge or positional depth. So the curriculum leads with a blunder-check
routine and focus habits (LR drives Lesson 0001) *before* investing heavily in openings.
Opening/tactics/endgame goals from [[MISSION.md]] are sequenced behind that foundation.
